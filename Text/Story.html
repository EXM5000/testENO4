Our Story

Ember & Oak was born from a curiosity for creation and a passion for business. Years ago, We discovered the joys and challenges of entrepreneurship through early ventures with friends, from a clothing brand called Loco to experimenting with innovative product ideas. One of our most memorable projects was Flame, a line of concrete candles.
Flame started as a simple idea: taking cheap, durable concrete and turning it into something beautiful. We experimented with molds, scents, and designs, learning firsthand the challenges of production - from jars that crumbled to dye that bled onto hands. Despite the setbacks, Flame taught us the importance of product quality, attention to detail, and resilience in running a business.
